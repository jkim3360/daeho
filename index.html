Welcome. Glad you stopped by.


Korea.

After graduating from New York University with a degree in education in 2008, I moved to South Korea as an expat teaching English for five years. It was a wonderful experience that showed me how large the world is how much we can learn from others.


Currently a Teacher and Aspiring Software Engineer.

I've been teaching the past ten years. I love working with youth, teaching, interacting with and cracking jokes with my students, but I have decided to shift gears to challenge different parts of my brain and grow as a person.


Coding and tech will bring out the best in me.
Although teaching is a rewarding and noble occupation, I have not been able to tap into my the technical side of my personality. I have a deep passion for learning and am good at systematically teaching myself new things, especially when I really like the subject. Coding is a job I believe I can excel in if I am given the right opportunities.


Musician.

I love music and taught myself guitar as a way to cope with pain. I studied music theory and played live with other musicians in the past. Now I play guitar in my bedroom as a creative outlet and stress reliever, but music will always be a part large part of my life.


Mom.

Yellow is the color of light and hope. If it weren't for this wonderful woman, I would not be half the person I am today. She gave up everything for our family and unfortunately left us too soon. I couldn't have asked for a better mother. Missing her very much!


Film and Video Games.

I love a good movie, tv show and occasional video game binge as much as anyone else. I was also a big fan of Final Fantasy, Metal Gear Solid, FIFA growing up. Always down for some good entertainment!
